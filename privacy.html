Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Summary

LocalQuill does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to any external server. Everything runs locally on your machine.

Data Collection

LocalQuill collects no personal data whatsoever. We do not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and never leaves your device:

· Your license key (for activation verification)

· Your persona text (writing style examples you provide)

· Your resume data (extracted text from uploaded resume)

· Writing history (last 50 results, stored locally)

· Preferences and settings

AI Processing

All AI processing is done locally on your machine via Ollama running at localhost. Your text, documents, and images are never sent to any external server or third party.

Network Requests

The only network request LocalQuill makes is to http://localhost:11434 — your own local Ollama instance running on your machine. One additional request is made to api.dodopayments.com solely to verify your license key at activation time.

Third Parties

LocalQuill does not share any data with third parties. No advertising, no analytics services, no data brokers.
